About Trusty

Dr. pallavi Naik: founder & director

Mrs.Dr. Pallavi Naik is a very promising and talented Bharatnatym dancer and is learning the Bharatnatym art form with a great dedication and sincerity for more than 6 years.She has received the Bharatnatym Nrityalankar degree from the Akhil Bharatiya Gandharva Mahavidyalay Mandal. She has performed in various Music and Dance festivals in mumbai & Pune

She is the Founder and Director of Kalapragan Trust which is her Bharatnatym Dance institute. Apart from being a very talented performer Mrs. Dr. Pallavi Naik is a very able Guru (teacher). The students are very fond of her and more than 200 students are receiving training in Bharatnatym at her institute.
